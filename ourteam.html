Meet Our Team

Tom West |

Managing Director

In his current role as a managing director of Twig Services, Tom has found a match made in heaven (yet another cliché!) as by offering the video-based and interactive state-of-the-art online staff training that he has at his disposal, he takes great pleasure in having lots of ‘happy bunnies’ as customers. He has the perfect tools of the trade to help him achieve these goals with the tailor-made discounted pricing packages available, which can not only help companies to maximise their efficiency, but it can also help them to keep their staff training costs low! He is forever reminiscing about the days though when he used to watch his beloved Chelsea play, albeit 2nd division football for a grand total of around £1.00 per day out which would include travel, admittance, and a match program! Tom welcomes any online staff training enquiries for companies up to and beyond 5,000 employees, that is of course, if you can put up with the odd cliché!

Nisha Parmar |

Senior Sales Consultant

‘Twig’ is very privileged to welcome Nisha to the team and are delighted to see that she has ‘hit the ground’ running in becoming a fully-fledged senior sales consultant in lightning-fast time! Coupled with her amazing energy levels, the company is quickly having to adapt to cope with the increased online staff training sales enquiries that this ‘one man’ or rather ‘one woman army’ is generating! The only issue we have with her, is that she is a passionate ‘United’ footie fan (Manchester of course!) which would normally be a huge bone of contention in the office with her director Tom who is a rival ‘Chelsea’ fan. We are pleased to report however, that so far, all is harmonious in the office and in fact an unlikely bond has been formed between the pair, mainly because both teams are playing so badly at the moment! Let’s just say that there are plenty of boxes of ‘Kleenex’ to-hand in the office because of their team’s awful ‘footie’ results, accompanied by the unsavoury and pungent smell, caused by lots of ‘crying over spilt milk!’ Nisha does not bite, you will be pleased to hear, so feel free to approach her for any of your online staff training enquiries and if your call should coincide with a ‘rare’ United win, you may even be able to negotiate some extra discounted pricing on our on your quotation!

Annie Cooper

Office Manager

Annie is an invaluable member of the Twig Services team and is the steady rock that gels the company and our very own inhouse staff training together! Annie has been a key part of many SMEs for most of her working career but please don’t be fooled by the soft-looking exterior as she rules with an ‘iron fist!’ We will not go as far as saying staff members ‘shake in their boots’ when she is around but let’s just say that the office is run in an orderly and ‘ship-shape Bristol Fashion’ when she is around and even directors are wary of her wrath! In her spare time, Annie loves DIY and thinks she is better than most men at it, although, she does not want to appear sexist, especially coming from a staff training HR background! She also loves cooking and getting her fingers green in her garden but only during the summer months as she ‘hibernates like an angry bear’ in the winter! If you have any issues you want to raise, speak to Annie, as there’s a good chance, she will be able to ‘crack a whip’ (literally!) and get them resolved but it is not advised to ask her for any discounted pricing if she gets out the wrong side of bed that morning!
