Universal Credit PAYG Plan

Note: This calculator doesn’t include PAYG as you only pay for what you need and therefore it doesn’t need a calculated price.

With our unique ‘Universal Credit’ (PAYG) scheme, you will not be restricted to nominate course topics ‘up-front’ in order to receive a discounted premium, as you can select from our entire catalogue of more than 100 course titles and can automatically enjoy discounted prices! You will also have the convenience and facility in place to allocate any more staff training that may be required on an ad-hoc basis and the funds can be ‘topped up’, if needed in the future, as you would do with a PAYG mobile phone tariff!


Unlimited Use (Full Access)

Our ‘Unlimited Use’ (Full access) subscription plan makes staff training cost effective and allows for budgets to be capped, as it grants organisations full access to distribute our entire catalogue of approved e-Learning training course videos (excluding the Level 1 H&S in a Construction Environment ‘supervised exam upgrade’) to their workforce for one flat rate!
