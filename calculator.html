Plan Information

Unlimited Use (Full Access)

Our ‘Unlimited Use’ (Full access) subscription plan makes staff training cost effective and allows for budgets to be capped, as it grants organisations full access to distribute our entire catalogue of approved e-Learning training course videos (excluding a few premium courses) to their workforce for one flat rate!

The prices quoted above are based on our INTEREST FREE monthly payment plan but DISCOUNTS ARE AVAILABLE TO COMPANIES WHO CAN PAY FOR THEIR SUBSCRIPTION PLAN IN ONE GO and quotations for THREE and FIVE year subscription plans are available upon request.


Universal Credit PAYG Plan

Note: The above calculator does not include tariffs for our PAYG (Universal Credit) plans, where top up credits start from £500.00 and are available upon request!

With our unique ‘Universal Credit’ (PAYG) scheme, you do not have to nominate what course topics are required ‘up-front’, as you can select from our entire catalogue of around 150 course titles and automatically enjoy discounted prices! Furthermore, you will also have the convenience and facility in place to allocate more staff training that may be required on an ad-hoc basis, and the funds can be ‘topped up’, if needed in the future, as you would do with a PAYG mobile phone tariff!
